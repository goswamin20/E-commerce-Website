Mahua Chaterjee

Designer Mahua Chatterjee grew up in Bhopal, India. Since her childhood, she always dreamt of puring a creative career. She loved sketching cartoons, dresses at a young age. All her notebooks will be filled with her little creations.

In 2009, Mahua moved to Symboisis Pune to study Fashion designing.

In 2013, after graduating she worked with Sabyachai and went for India Lakme Fashion Week that year.

After gaining experience she started free lancing and still continues to do so.
